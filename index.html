We are the Lambda Chapter of alpha Kappa Delta Phi!

alpha Kappa Delta Phi is an Asian interest sorority whose pillars focus on scholarship, leadership, sisterhood, and a philanthropy of breast cancer awareness. With over 50 chapters, we are the largest and fastest growing Asian interest sorority on the country. Explore the website or check us out on social media to learn more!
